About MGU Helper

M.G.U. Helper is a mobile app designed specifically for MG University students. The app offers an easy way to access previous year question papers, helping students prepare effectively for their exams.   • Easy Navigation: The user-friendly interface ensures that students can quickly find the materials they need.
  • Free Access: All materials are freely available to students, helping to ensure that resources are accessible to everyone.

About the Developer

M.G.U. Helper is developed by ASK Productions, an organization committed to enhancing educational opportunities by providing free learning resources to students. Our mission is to make education more accessible to everyone, starting with our focus on MG University students.

Need Help with Doubts? Wikipedia API is Here to Assist You!

We understand that students often have questions or encounter difficulties while studying. To provide assistance, MGU Helper integrates the Wikipedia API directly into the app. The Wikipedia API serves as a virtual assistant, helping you find relevant articles and answers to clarify doubts related to the subjects you're studying.

The Wikipedia API integration within MGU Helper allows students to access vast academic knowledge stored in Wikipedia. Students can ask questions about any academic topic, and the Wikipedia API will fetch detailed and informative articles to support learning.

What is the Wikipedia API? The Wikipedia API allows access to the vast knowledge stored in Wikipedia. It provides students with detailed articles and reliable information to help answer a wide range of academic questions, from definitions to complex explanations.

How does it work? When you have a question or need clarification, you can simply ask within the app, and the Wikipedia API will provide you with relevant article summaries, helping you clear any doubts you may have.
